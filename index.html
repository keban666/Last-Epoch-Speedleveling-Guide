Last Epoch Speedleveling Guide

This guide details a highly optimized route to take a fresh character from level 1 all the way to early endgame content (Monoliths) in just over an hour and a half. The method leverages dungeon skip mechanic (the Temporal Sanctum) and efficient side quest routing to pick up passive points and idol slots without fully clearing the campaign.

Damage Mitigation: As you progress prioritize Physical Resistance initially.
              Transition into Void Resistance for Act II and Act III.
              Switch to Necrotic Resistance around Act IV as you face increased necrotic damage.
